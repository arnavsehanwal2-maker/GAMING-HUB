Best Free Fire Tips for Beginners

Free Fire is one of the most popular battle royale games on mobile. Beginners often struggle with survival, aiming, and map awareness. Learning a few simple strategies can quickly improve your gameplay and help you win more matches.

Choose the Right Landing Spot

Avoid crowded areas at the start. Landing in quieter zones gives you time to collect weapons and gear before fighting enemies.

Use Headphones

Sound is very important in Free Fire. Footsteps and gunshots help you detect enemies nearby and prepare for combat.

Adjust Sensitivity Settings

Proper sensitivity improves aiming accuracy and makes headshots easier. Experiment with settings until you find comfortable controls.

Stay Inside the Safe Zone

Always watch the map and move early toward the safe zone to avoid damage and enemy ambushes.

Practice Regularly

Consistent practice is the best way to improve skills, reaction time, and strategy.

By following these beginner tips, you can survive longer and enjoy Free Fire more effectively.

BGMI Best Sensitivity Settings Guide

Having the right sensitivity settings in BGMI can greatly improve your aiming and recoil control. Many professional players customize their sensitivity to achieve smooth movement and accurate shots.

Camera Sensitivity

Set camera sensitivity high enough for quick movement but stable enough to track enemies smoothly.

ADS Sensitivity

ADS sensitivity should allow controlled spraying without excessive recoil. Balanced settings help in mid-range fights.

Gyroscope Usage

Using gyroscope improves precision while spraying and tracking enemies. Practice in training mode to master it.

Test in Training Ground

Always test new sensitivity settings before ranked matches to avoid performance issues.

Perfect sensitivity depends on your device and playstyle, so adjust gradually until comfortable.
